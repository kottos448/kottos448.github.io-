What is This Ability Chat?

This Ability Chat aims to be a fun and safe space to share and discuss stories of 'handicapable' trials, tribulations and triumphs, starting with my own. I'm very excited to build this from the ground up (and I'm doing it from scratch, so it might take a while) and I would love any input and suggestions, so feel free to send me a message.

I also see a wonderful potential for This Ability Chat to grow into a collective think-tank of sorts and serve as a platform for discovering accessibility innovations and inventions.
